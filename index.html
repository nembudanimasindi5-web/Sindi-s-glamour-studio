About Us

BRIEF HISTORY

Sindi’s glamour studio was launched when the owner was learning more about modern beauty standards. It started as a nail bar, but it grew more into a hair and nail studio

MISSION STATEMENT

Sindi’s glamour studio aims to make clients feel great about themselves, enhance one’s confidence and make clients feel free to communicate their dissatisfactions

VISION STATEMENT

SGS vision is to grow more and not only offer service to female but also become a unisex studio where male haircuts are provided. SGS aims to sell hair products in the future.

Team Members/ staff

Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to providing exceptional beauty services and creating a welcoming environment for all clients.
